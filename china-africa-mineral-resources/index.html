China dominates global demand for Africa's mineral resources

Despite US' concern about China's increasing influence in Africa, China's position as the continent's largest trading partner, especially as a buyer of the continent's mineral resources continue to outstrip other countries'.

Experts say the recent visit to Africa by the US Secretary of State Antony Blinken and his promise that President Biden would host a US-Africa Leaders Summit is part of effort to curtail China's increasing influence on the continent.

From Ghana to Guinea and Zimbabwe to South Africa, China holds significant influence in mining and importing most of the continent's base metals and mineral resources.

China imports most of the chromium, nickel, iron ore and cobalt produced in Africa with significant share of global import of platinum from Africa.
